Library Management System


A college library management is a project that manages and stores books information electronically according to students needs. The system helps both students and library manager to keep a constant track of all the books available in the library. It allows both the admin and the student to search for the desired book. It becomes necessary for colleges to keep a continuous check on the books issued and returned and even calculate fine. This task if carried out manually will be tedious and includes chances of mistakes. These errors are avoided by allowing the system to keep track of information such as issue date, last date to return the book and even fine information and thus there is no need to keep manual track of this information which thereby avoids chances of mistakes. Thus this system reduces manual work to a great extent allows smooth flow of library activities by removing chances of errors in the details.A Library Management System is a software built to handle the primary housekeeping functions of a library. Libraries rely on library management systems to manage asset collections as well as relationships with their members. Library management systems help libraries keep track of the books and their checkouts, as well as members‚Äô subscriptions and profiles. Library management systems also involve maintaining the database for entering new books and recording books that have been borrowed with their respective due dates.

Features of LMS





Barcode scanner

Barcode feature is introduced in this library management system. This will help to save time and keep the track for the books s records in computer. User can scan the books and retrieve the information related to it. Identifying different materials will be easy using barcode scanner.




More Books

More number of books are introduced in this system. Students from different faculty can choose various course books from different publications and writers. Besides course books, students are provided with numerous novels, journals, magazines, notes, newpapers, articles and many more.




Fines & Late Fees

Students are notified with a message about the late fees and fines with their registered email id or mobile number. Students can pay their fines from online payment method like e-sewa. A prior message will be send to students before their book submission date.




Order Online

Students or readers can order book online so that library staff can arrange their ordered materials as early as possible. This will reduce the time required for searching the books.
